# Logo Usage

## Primary Logo

Use `landmarksignal-logo-dark.svg` on light backgrounds and `landmarksignal-logo-light.svg` on dark navy backgrounds.

## Icon

Use the icon when the full wordmark is too wide, such as favicon-style placements, square avatars, or compact product surfaces.

## Product Lockups

Use product lockups when presenting the LandmarkSignal product family:

- Runtime: governed operational foundation
- Radar: visibility and operational signals
- Shield: protective controls and policy enforcement

## Clear Space

Keep clear space around the logo equal to at least the width of the beacon stem. Do not crowd it with borders, text, product UI, or partner marks.

## Do Not

- Do not add gradients, glows, shadows, or neon effects.
- Do not redraw the mark as a brain, circuit, orb, or abstract AI symbol.
- Do not stretch, skew, rotate, or crop the mark.
- Do not recolor outside the approved color system.
- Do not convert text lockups to raster source files.
